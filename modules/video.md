## video

Video element

    <module type="video" />

## Examples

#### Display video from url

    <?php //show video ?>
    <module type="video" url="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iWjRsd4kk9w" />

#### Set the video size

    <?php //show video ?>
    <module type="video" url="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=630ajZaox-g" width="500" height="350" />
